About The Golden City Township

The Golden City, a 171.32-acre mixed-use freehold township in IMT Manesar, rises at the confluence of three major highways. It comprises industrial, residences and commercial spaces.

➤

Golden Industrial Zone, spread over more than 95 acres, offers freehold industrial plots ranging from 512 sq. m. to 5 acres.

➤

The Golden Residences, offering plots ranging from 126 sq. m. to 150 sq. m. and 3 BHK premium low-rise residences, span across an area of 22 acres, lined with greenery and full of open spaces.

➤

The soon-to-be-launched add 53 acres of wellness-focused low-rise community living. These developments are seamlessly complemented, an upcoming commercial development, which will offer over 16 lakh sq. ft. of upscale retail and dining.
